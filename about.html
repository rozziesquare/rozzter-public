About Rozzter

Rozzter delivers fast lookup of American football players by name, team, and jersey number. Identify who wears a number or quickly check a name. No account and no ads.

Highlights

Fast name, team, and jersey number lookup. Recent history for instant reopen. Dark and light themes (auto-saved). Clean, tap-friendly design. Local only: minimal preference storage.

How It Works

Search by player name. Or select a team, enter a jersey number, and launch the browser query. Use the Recent tab to jump back to past lookups.

Privacy

We do not show ads or share personal information with advertisers. We only store simple preferences (theme choice and recent lookups) on your device; nothing is sent to a server. External results open in your browser. If we ever introduce ads in the future, we will update this description and the privacy policy before they appear.

Roadmap

More sports and advanced roster features are planned as optional in-app purchases. Stay tuned.

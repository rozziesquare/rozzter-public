Privacy Policy - Rozzter

Effective date: 2025-11-14

Summary

Rozzter is a lightweight lookup tool for NFL players. The app stores limited preferences and history only on your device (e.g., recent searches, your last selected team, and your theme preference). Rozzter does not transmit this data to the developer or any third party.

What data Rozzter collects

  • In‑app search history: Recent lookup queries you enter (player name or team + jersey number). Stored locally on the device only.
  • Last selected team: The most recent team you picked, so the app can reopen with that team preselected. Stored locally on the device only.
  • Theme preference: Your light/dark theme choice. Stored locally on the device only.

Rozzter does not collect personal information, contact information, unique device identifiers, precise location, or any analytics/advertising data.

How we use this data

Local data is used only to power in‑app features like the Recents list, remembering your last selected team, and your preferred theme. We do not use this data for analytics, advertising, profiling, or sharing with third parties.

Storage and security

Your recent searches, team preference, and theme setting are stored locally in the app’s storage on your device. Rozzter does not upload this information to any server. When Rozzter opens search results or third‑party pages (for example, ESPN or Google), it launches your device’s external web browser using HTTPS links; any data collected by those sites is governed by their privacy policies, not Rozzter’s.

Backups: On Android devices, your system or cloud backup may include Rozzter’s local app data (such as recent searches and preferences) associated with your Google account. Backups are managed by your device/Google account and are not transmitted to the Rozzter developer.

Data deletion

You can remove locally stored data at any time:

  • Use the app’s Recent → Clear Recent control to clear recent searches.
  • To remove all local app data (including last selected team and theme), uninstall the app or clear its storage via: Device Settings → Apps → Rozzter → Storage → Clear data.

Rozzter has no user accounts and no server-side data; we do not store any data remotely. Because we do not store any data remotely, data deletion requests are not applicable.

Third‑party websites and content

Rozzter constructs and opens HTTPS URLs in your external browser to display search results on third‑party sites (for example, ESPN or Google). Rozzter does not embed or scrape third‑party content. Any data those third parties collect when you use your browser is subject to their privacy policies.

Children

Rozzter is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, please email contact@rozziesquare.com

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The “Effective date” above will be updated when material changes are made.
